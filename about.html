About Nader Health & Life Advocacy & Consulting

We support individuals and families through complex medical and life challenges with clarity, organization, and compassionate advocacy.

Cynthia Nader, RN – CEO & Chief Advocate

With nearly five decades in nursing, clinical management, quality improvement, risk management, and patient advocacy, Cynthia helps clients understand their options, protect their safety, and avoid costly system errors.

  • Medical & clinical navigation
  • Hospital systems support and discharge planning
  • Billing accuracy and financial navigation
  • Family communication and life-event support

Patrick Kincaid – COO & Strategic Advisor

Patrick oversees the operational and strategic backbone of the firm. He supports systems, website development, client-education tools, research, and special projects so Cynthia can focus fully on advocacy and client outcomes.

  • Operations, workflow, and internal systems
  • Website and digital infrastructure
  • Client education tools and content development
  • Project coordination and research
