Frequently Asked Questions

What does a health & life advocate do?

We help you navigate complex medical and life situations by organizing information, coordinating next steps, and supporting communication and decision-making.

Do you work virtually?

Yes. Virtual support is available, and in-person options may be offered depending on location and situation.

Do you accept insurance?

Services are typically private-pay. We can discuss options during your discovery call.

What is the first step?

Start with a discovery call. We’ll learn your situation and recommend the best next step.
